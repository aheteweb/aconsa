We export fresh and frozen seafood.

About us

Aconsa is a young company, dedicated to the export of fresh and frozen fish and seafood, both imported and domestic.

In order to satisfy the requirements of the most demanding markets, we've implemented strict controls in all stages of our production process which allows us to ensure the highest quality of our final products.

All of our products are processed in our own facilities, under strict quality and healthy controls.

Our facilities

Our processing plant is strategically located in the city of Cumana-Venezuela where fishing represents one of the main economic activities, this allows us to process a great variety and quantity of fresh and frozen seafood.

All of our processing rooms hold refrigeration systems in order to maintain the necessary cold chain in all of the processing stages.

Service

We are a young, dynamic and experienced team, always willing to provide our customers with the required services.

We accompany and advise our clients in all of the process stages, from the selection to product delivery.

Quality

We process the best fish and seafood, our products are preserved with the latest technology, which allows us to maintain intact the product's qualities from their origin.

There are expert technicians within our team, responsible for controlling product reception and unloading, paying special attention to the product's status, temperature and expiration dates. Likewise, product manipulation within our facilities is carefully monitored.

Distribution

We ensure a fast and safe distribution using our own refrigerated transportation, which complies with the requirements established by regulatory entities, thus ensuring the cold chain of all of our products.

Our products are under exhaustive temperature, handling and sanitary controls during the distribution, this allows us to guarantee the delivering of a product with its intact qualities, just as if it had just left the sea.
